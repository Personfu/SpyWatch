# Illegals and cover-legend roster notes

SpyWatch keeps this roster as a public-source research aid, not as a people-tracking product. The goal is to help students and defenders understand how long-term cover legends, access routes, aliases, and public attribution claims are documented.

## Safety boundary

Do not add private home addresses, private family details, private social-media material, scraped dating-profile images, unverified birth dates, live contact details, or instructions for finding people in real time. For pictures, link to official case pages, court exhibits, reputable news image pages, or Wikimedia-style public media pages. Do not copy private images into the repository.
